Package Details

The following lists details what each package includes:

Basic
The perfect choice for routine care and maintenance to keep your dog looking and feeling their best. Our basic grooming package includes a thorough bath using high-quality, gentle shampoos, followed by a blow-dry and brush-out to remove loose hair and tangles. We also provide ear cleaning, nail trimming, and a sanitary trim to ensure your pet stays clean and comfortable between grooming sessions.
Premium
Our premium grooming package offers everything in the basic package, with a few extra touches for dogs who need a little more pampering. This package includes a customized haircut to match your dog's breed or your personal preference, along with conditioning treatments to leave their coat shiny and soft. We finish with a spritz of dog-safe cologne and a cute accessory like a bow or bandana for that extra flair.
Deluxe
Treat your dog to the ultimate spa experience with our deluxe grooming package. In addition to all the services in the premium package, we include a deep-cleaning exfoliating bath, a teeth cleaning to maintain oral hygiene, and a relaxing paw pad treatment to soothe and protect your pet's paws. Your dog will leave feeling refreshed, pampered, and ready to show off their new look!
